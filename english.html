Our Methodology

All of our projects Website development go through a few steps to ensure you are satisfied with the final product.

Architecture

Our first step is to study the reading flow of the content.

prototyping

After validating the flow, we design a prototype of your landing page.

Frontend

Based on the designed screens, we programmed the visual part of the landing page.

Integration

Then we integrate the forms with your platform.

homologation

After completing the previous steps, we publish the landing page in a private place for evaluation.

Delivery

When everything is tested and approved, we publish your landing page at the final address.

Before publication, each page of the site goes through a checklist that includes:
Review of links, functionalities, tests on smartphones and browsers.
